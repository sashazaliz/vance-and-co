The Vance Way

NO
SHORT
CUTS.

W

e opened in 2009 with a single thesis: the residential construction industry had a quality problem that no one was willing to name out loud. Timelines were fiction. Budgets were estimates that contractors had no intention of honoring. The "design" in design-build usually meant someone's brother-in-law had an opinion.

Vance & Co. was built around a different idea. Every project has a fixed-scope contract. Every timeline has a daily accountability log. Every material decision comes with a documented alternative and a price difference. Our clients know exactly what they're getting before the first nail is pulled.

We are selective. We take on approximately 18 projects per year, which means we are never overextended, never shuffling crews between sites, never treating your home as a low-priority job. When you hire Vance & Co., you are one of eighteen families we are responsible to that year. That is the relationship.

01
FIXED-SCOPE CONTRACTS

We don't give ranges and hope for the best. Every engagement begins with a detailed scope document. Price changes only when the scope changes — and only with written client approval.

02
IN-HOUSE DESIGN

Our design team works in the same building as our build team. When design and construction are siloed, problems are discovered on-site. We find them on paper, before anything costs money to fix.

03
DAILY SITE REPORTS

Every client receives a daily progress photo and written summary. Not weekly. Not "we'll call you if there's an issue." Daily. Because it's your home and you deserve to know what's happening in it.

04
2-YEAR WARRANTY

Every project comes with a 2-year comprehensive warranty on workmanship. Not the industry-standard 1-year. Not a warranty that requires six phone calls to activate. Two years, in writing, starting at final walkthrough.

How It Works

ZERO
SUR
PRIS
ES.

01
DISCOVERY CALL

A 30-minute call with our project director to understand your scope, timeline, and budget range. We will tell you immediately if your project is a fit for us and if our typical cost range aligns with your expectations. We don't waste your time with a site visit if the numbers won't work.

Week 1 · 30 Minutes
02
SITE ASSESSMENT & SCOPE

Our design-build team visits the property, documents existing conditions, and develops a detailed written scope of work. This document drives everything — design decisions, material selections, subcontractor bids, timeline. It takes 2–3 weeks to produce properly and it is not a shortcut we take.

Weeks 2–4 · Site Visit + Documentation
03
FIXED-SCOPE CONTRACT

You receive a single document with the complete scope, a line-item budget with no ranges, a day-by-day project schedule, and the terms of the 2-year workmanship warranty. You sign one contract. You don't hear "unforeseen conditions" as a reason to increase costs unless something genuinely unforeseen happens — and we define what that means in writing.

Week 4–5 · Contract Execution
04
BUILD & DAILY REPORTING

Work begins on the contracted start date. Every day, your project manager sends a photo report and written summary of completed work, next day's activities, and any open items requiring your input. You are never waiting for a call back. You are never wondering what's happening in your home.

Per Contract Schedule · Daily Updates
05
FINAL WALKTHROUGH & WARRANTY

Every project ends with a joint walkthrough with your project director. A punch list is generated, resolved within 10 business days, and signed off by you. Your 2-year warranty begins at your signature. Our service team remains reachable by direct phone line — not a ticketing system, not an email queue.

Final Week · Warranty Begins Day 1
